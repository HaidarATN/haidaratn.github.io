About Me

My name is Haidar Abhirama, an AR/VR developer from Indonesia with more than 4 years of experience across a broad range of game and AR/VR development field.

I've graduate from Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya (The Electronic Engineering Polytechnic Institute of Surabaya) of Game Technology subject with a Bachelor’s degree of Applied Computer and I've developed various AR/VR software for various devices and platform such as Mobile, Google Cardboard, Microsoft Hololens, HTC Vive, Oculus Quest, and Oculus Rift.
